About Me

I’m Darshan Sonavane, a passionate Web Developer with a Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology and a Diploma in Computer Science. With hands-on experience from internships at Codeclause and OasisInfobyte, I have developed projects like AgriMind: Precision Farming with Machine Learning, Online Vegetables Ordering System, and Expense Tracker System.

Skilled in C, C++, Java, and web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and MySQL, I am committed to creating intuitive and user-friendly websites. I hold certifications in Web Development, Google Data Analytics, and UI/UX Design. My project AgriMind won the runner-up prize at GAGNANT 2024 and is copyrighted by the Government of India. I’m also passionate about cycling, cricket,and music, where I share my insights on various topics.
